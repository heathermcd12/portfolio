Hi there / Bonjour!

A bit about me!

I'm Heather Lynn McDonell, an unruly-haired, bi-lingual Graphic Design student in her final year at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. I am passionate about motion graphics, illustration, branding and printmaking. I love working with my hands to design, whether it be using a letterpress to make a poster or creating stop motion for a video project.

Outside of design, I have hobbies too! I care deeply about the environment. I love to spend days outdoors, make floral arrangements and play with animals. I bake a lot, take printmaking classes and love telling stories.

Work Experience

I have experience working in design studios such as Greenmelon Inc and Anick Bauer Design. This knowledge has helped me understand which talents I should focus on and how to collaborate with other designers to create meaningful design.

Clients
