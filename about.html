A bit about me!

I'm Heather Lynn, an unruly-haired, bi-lingual graphic design student in her final year of Graphic Design at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. Currently on the search of an internship that will fulfill my need to be hands-on with projects and allow my creativity to roam. Passionate about art direction, motion graphics, illustration and printmaking. I love working with my hands to mix art and design, whether it be using a letterpress to make a poster or cutting up jeans for an editorial.

Outside of design, I have hobbies too! I love to spend days outdoors, make floral arrangements and play with animals. I bake a lot, take printmaking classes and love telling stories.
