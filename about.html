Salut!

I'm Heather Lynn McDonell, an unruly-haired, bilingual Graphic Design student in her final year at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. I am passionate about art direction, motion graphics, illustration and branding. I design things that are dramatic, meaningful and believe in creating for the greater good.

Outside of design, I have hobbies too! I love to spend days outdoors, watch old films, make floral arrangements and tell interesting stories.
