Audrey & Gap

Background

In 2006, GAP used old footage of the late Audrey Hepburn for a campaign promoting their Skinny Black Pant. Due to the company's inappropriate use of a dead celebrity to boost its sales, it was not well received.

Concept

An artsy revolt against the company, using their product. To make the message more meaningful, I shredded and ripped apart a pair of black GAP jeans, to rebuild them into an impactful design.

Process

I laid out the black shreds to form letters and words. After the layout was finished, I photographed the design and edited the photos to created a higher contrast.
