Begin Again

Begin Again is an experimental motion piece that I did in honour of all that is spooky. I used the 3-D camera in After-Effects to create the zooming in and out in this video, to add drama and depth to the story.

Concept

Halloween night, the viewer is taken on a floating adventure through an old house by an unlikely entrance. What will happen next?

Colour

I am not a big fan of colour at times, and in this case I wanted it to be only black and white, with yellow as an accent.
