Exhibition Art Book - BONES2

Client: OLICORNO
Art Direction/Design/Typography: Heather Lynn

BONES2 is an exhibition and art book created for Montreal based artist OLICORNO. In order to create a unique exhibition experience, I incorporated large typography and circles to frame his artwork.

To recreate that experience, I made an artist book that was not bound at the spine which made it easy for the reader to look at each work of art seperately, like being in the gallery.
