Strong lines

This illustration series focuses on strong and confident women that inspire me. The drawings are all done by hand with faber-castell markers. The lines are a mixture of delicate and bold, just like each of the women I look up to.

Catwoman circa 1966

This powerful villain kicks butt and sports killer brows. Her pointy eyebrows have helped develop my illustration style and are seen in many of my drawings.

Bettie Page

Bettie, as many other old school stars, fought her way to the top. She taught women everywhere to not be ashamed of who you are and to be confident in yourself.
