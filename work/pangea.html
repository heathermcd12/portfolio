SUMMER CAMPAIGN

Client: Pangea
Design: Veronique Lafortune
Art Direction/Illustration/Design: Heather Lynn

Simple is good. That’s what our campaign embodies: minimalist, practical and fun design. Every seam, every detail and every line is thoughtfully designed to catch the eye without being too bold.

Founded by Karina K in 2013 in Little Burgundy, one of Montreal’s most creative neighborhoods, Pangea incarnates youth, imagination and fun with a touch of tropical flavor.
