Rocky Horror Revival

Rocky Horror has always held a place in my heart. The iconic franchise turned 40 this year, reminding fans of how time may pass but classics stay classic. To celebrate this anniversary, I gave the brand a fresh look.

Concept

A redesign of their album cover that would not include Magenta's iconic red lips, but still be recognizable.

Colour

To keep the colour scheme gaudy just like the characters' costumes, I used CMYK with 3 Pantones. The mixture worked well, embodying Rocky Horrors tacky esthetic.
