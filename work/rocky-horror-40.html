It's not easy having a good time.

Rocky Horror has always held a place in my heart. The iconic franchise turned 40 this year, reminding fans of how time may pass but classics are popular forever. To celebrate this anniversary, I gave the cult classic a fresh look.

A redesign of their album cover and logo that would not include Magenta's iconic red lips, but still be recognizable. To keep the colour scheme gaudy just like the characters' costumes, I used bright colours (CMYK). The mixture worked well, embodying Rocky Horror's tacky aesthetic.
