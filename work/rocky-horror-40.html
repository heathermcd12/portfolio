Rocky Horror Revival

Rocky Horror has always held a place in my heart. Its characters are timeless and its music will forever be played over and over on Halloween. The iconic franchise turned 40 this year, reminding fans of how time may pass but classics stay classic. To celebrate this anniversary, I gave the brand a fresh look.

Concept

A redesign of their album cover that would not include Magenta's iconic red lips, but still be recognizable.

Colour

To keep the colour scheme gaudy just like the characters' costumes, I used CMYK with 3 Pantones. The mixture worked well, embodying Rocky Horrors tacky esthetic.

The poster can be seen hanging on The Record Centers wall! If you're in Ottawa, come check it out!
