Adekato - Logo design

Client

Adekato, an artist run studio in Ottawa, Canada needed a new look. The studio is known for their beautiful watercolour paintings, which inspired the hand done, brush wordmark.

Art Direction

Under the direction of Taulant Sulko, over a hundred designs were all hand done quick and fluidly. Doing this ensured that no two designs were alike or overthought. Executed with indian ink and chinese brushes, the wordmark is organic and unique.
