Personal brand: Heather Lynn

Art Direction

This brand is an artsy and crafty approach to self-promotion, focusing on what I love most: printmaking and illustration. Inspired by gothic, art deco typography and hand crafted design, I created a wordmark that embodied both my personality and style. Supporting the logo are printed pieces meant to start a conversation, each done by myself with a Risograph printer or stamped by hand.

Colour

Soft and cheery, these colours balance well with the highly chromatic blue of the logo. The printed materials are meant to complement the website, creating a recognizable brand.
