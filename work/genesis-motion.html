Genesis

Concept

Body language speaks louder than words. My vision for this video was to tell a story with illustrated type being revealed by the model's hand.

Process

This stop motion video was filmed in my tiny apartment, using only natural light. Between taking each photo I would add a word to the model's arm/body and reposition her hand. After the filming was done, I edited in Lightroom and animated it in After Effects.
