Bilingual Cirque du Soleil booklet

Art Direction

An energetic and creative take on Cirque du Soleil's annual report. I focused the direction of this project around the words "eerie" and "mysterious", two words I associate with Cirque du Soleil. Using original illustrations, the annual report resembles a storybook that engages all ages. I chose the font Penumbra Sans because of its pointy edges, and Adobe Garamond Pro for for its readability.

Colour

Inspired by the companies eerily beautiful stories and splashes of bright colours. In order to make the booklet not overwhelming, I only used yellow for colour. This creates a focus on not only the intricate illustrations, but the text.
