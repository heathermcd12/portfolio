MIX app

An application for artists of different mediums which helps them mix their paints, inks...etc. The app is clean and minimal in design, with a straight forward story to help the user walk through easily.

Concept

A clean ios app that is for artists that need help mixing their paints and inks. Each medium has a different consistency and chroma, so it was best that I gave the user the option to specify what medium they are working in.

Colour

To keep the colours to a minimum, I used only the light teal and black and white for type and iconography.
