Sketch - Framework

Role: Creative/Art Direction & storyboarding.

Concept

A promo video for Framework, Algonquin College's 2016 Graphic Design Grad Show. This video focuses on the first step of a brand, the conceptual and sketching phase.

Process

We wanted to walk the audience through the "behind the scenes" in the first phase of creating of a brand. Doing so, we included the original "pitches" for Grad Show's theme and logo.
