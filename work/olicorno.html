OLICORNO, 2013-2016

Client: OLICORNO
Art Direction/Design/Typography: Heather Lynn

Minimal and mysterious, that is what Olicorno - performance and visual artist - had in mind when we designed his first portfolio book.

Olicorno's art is bold, colourful and very conceptual. To incorporate large amounts of bilingual text, I made a layout that is easy to read with a natural flow throughout the book.
